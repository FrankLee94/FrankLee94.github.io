Short Bio

I am a postdoc researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF) with Yiting Xia and Anja Feldmann. Before joining MPI-INF, I received my B.E. and Ph.D. degree in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016 and 2021, respectively. I was advised by Prof. Xiaoping Zheng and Prof. Bingkun Zhou.

My research focuses on computer networks and data center networks, with my work published in top venues such as SIGCOMM, ToN, JOCN, and OFC. I has authored over 20 academic papers and served on program committees for conferences and as a reviewer for several journals. I has received the Tsinghua University Comprehensive Excellence Scholarship and the Tsinghua University Friends - Xiaomi Scholarship.
