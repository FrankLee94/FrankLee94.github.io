Short Bio

I am a postdoc researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Yiting Xia and Anja Feldmann. Prior to joining MPI-INF, I received my B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016 and 2021, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaoping Zheng and Prof. Bingkun Zhou.

My research focuses on computer networks and data center networks. My work has been published in top venues such as SIGCOMM, ToN, JOCN, and OFC. I have authored over 20 academic papers, served on program committees for conferences, and reviewed for several journals. I have been awarded the Tsinghua University Comprehensive Excellence Scholarship and the Tsinghua University Friends - Xiaomi Scholarship.
