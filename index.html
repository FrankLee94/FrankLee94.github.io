Short Bio

I am an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen University of Advanced Technology (SUAT). Prior to this, I was a postdoc researcher at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), working with Yiting Xia and Anja Feldmann (ACM Fellow). I received my B.E. and Ph.D. degrees in Electronic Engineering from Tsinghua University in 2016 and 2021, respectively, under the supervision of Xiaoping Zheng and Bingkun Zhou (CAS Fellow).

My research focuses on networking for ML and data center networks. My work has been published in top venues such as SIGCOMM, ToN, JOCN, and OFC. I have authored over 20 academic papers, served on program committees for conferences, and reviewed for several journals. I have been awarded the Tsinghua University Comprehensive Excellence Scholarship and the Tsinghua University Friends - Xiaomi Scholarship.
